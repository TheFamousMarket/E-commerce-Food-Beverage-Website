Data Deletion Instructions

Your privacy matters. If you wish to request deletion of your personal data from our systems (including data collected via Facebook or WhatsApp integration), please follow these steps:

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Send an email to support@thefmsmkt.com with the subject: “Data Deletion Request”.
  2. Include your full name, email address, and any relevant account or transaction info so we can verify your identity.
  3. We will respond within 7 business days to confirm your request and process the deletion.

Notes

Data deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed once processed. This includes order history, contact records, and account details.

For any questions, email support@thefmsmkt.com.
